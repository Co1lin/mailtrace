Stock batch

Generate IMbs in bulk for known recipients — one row per recipient with a count. They land in Stock state (no polling). Print them onto sticker sheets, peel-and-stick to envelopes as you go, then click Mark mailed on the piece (or in bulk) when you actually drop one in the box.

Order: if a row's Count > 1, copies are interleaved across rows (A, B, C, A, B, C…) instead of stacked (A, A, …, B, B, …). That way a single sticker sheet ends up with all your recipients — no more carrying ten pages just to mail to ten different people.
